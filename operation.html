Our flagship 12 MW air-cooled facility in Monahans, just west of Odessa, Texas, sets the standard for scalable compute facilities in the Permian Basin. Strategically positioned within ERCOT's robust energy grid, our facility harnesses an increasingly renewable energy mix, powering the most diverse fleet of next-generation ASIC miners.

From Bitcoin and Litecoin to Dogecoin and Kaspa, our advanced modular containers deliver exceptional performance even under extreme West Texas heat conditions. Serving hundreds of retail clients, Monahans offers unparalleled scalability, energy efficiency, and reliability, perfectly designed to meet the evolving demands of scalable compute facilities
